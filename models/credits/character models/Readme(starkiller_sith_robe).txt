Starkiller from The Force Unleashed wearing him Sith Robe. Includes SP, MP, and NPC support

To use this mod, place the "Starkiller_Sith_robe.pk3" file in the LucasArts\Star Wars Jedi Knight Jedi Academy\GameData\base, folder.

NPCS:
Starkiller_sith_robe(main model)
Starkiller_sith_robe_backhand(main model with Starkiller stance and backhand saber)


I believe this is the saber that the model uses https://jkhub.org/files/file/693-sith-stalker-single-saber/
